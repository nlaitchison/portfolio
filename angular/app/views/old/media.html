How Parkour Saved My Life - Parkour and Freerunning Documentary

“Dear Viewers, In this video you will learn about many things that effect my life on a daily basis. You will learn about my thoughts on training and my passion for movement. From seeing this I don’t want you to leave without receiving the message I am trying to portray. The overall theme I try to express here is to follow not only your dreams, but also your heart. Whatever may makes you happy, is what you should follow. So as you watch and enjoy this video about my own personal experience with “the pursuit of happiness,” I beg you to think deeply about your own pursuit and what life may have in store for you. Who knows? Maybe you'll change the world one day."

Much Love,

Casey Jones

As you know, it's our goal here at No Anchor to share videos that showcase the amazing talents of amazing athletes that we've had the privilege to meet in our travels. Sometimes, however, the story behind someone's incredible talent is every bit as amazing as the talent itself. This is the case with our good friend Casey Jones. We hope you will enjoy his message & let it inspire you as much as it has inspired us!

Tricking on Daytona Beach With No Anchor

Daytona Beach. We at No Anchor really really wanted to do a tricking video on the beach, and what better beach to do it at than Daytona!! So the night before we pressed out some Awesome No Anchor OG tank tops and prepared for the trip. We started the day off with the hour long drive in Ed’s truck, packing cameras, glidecams, and Camera accessories into the back seat and then we headed out. Picking up Shane and Ben on the way, we blared sweet tunes on the radio all the way to the beach. When we finally arrived in Daytona we got out and immediately me and Ben stretched up and prepared for what was going to be an amazing day of filming, flipping, and accomplishment. We made our way to the sand doing a few flips along the way before setting up at a spot directly under a Daytona Beach sign. There we got a few cinematic shots and a few clips of me and Ben flipping and running to the beach, Ben started the accomplishments of the day by doing a “standing Grand Master Swipe” on concrete for his first time, I proceeded to follow by doing a “standing full Double leg” on concrete for my first time. We then made our way to the “strip” of Daytona, where we did some cool combos in front of the local Go-Kart track. I tried a “terada full Double leg” for my first time ever and Ben did his first “full Snapu” on concrete!! It was a SICK shot and it took him quite a few takes to land, but when he finally did we all cheered and shared in his achievement. Then we moved along to go get some food, but we ran into Daytona Parkour! The local freerunning group. So we sat around and did some awesome wall runs with them before going to get ourselves some delicious grub. We ended up eating at a pizza shop that’s next to a cinema, this is when I learned of Edwin’s love for Jalapeno peppers. (had to add that in there). Anyway! Then we met up with NuGround, a group of trickers, acrobats, and break dancers. We filmed some amazing shot in the water and had an amazing tricking session. Ed and Shane enjoyed taking pictures and videos with the awesome lighting that was happening at that time, and also with the water which was looking GREAT on film. Some of our other accomplishments for the day were, my first “terada double full” and Ben landed some sweet spins also. All in all the Day was one of the most fun and exciting days of filming ive had with my great friends at No Anchor, who are always a pleasure to film, hang, and talk to. Love you guys!

Casey Jones, No Anchor Athlete.
