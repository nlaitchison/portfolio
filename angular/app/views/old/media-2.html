Weekends by No Anchor - Surfing in Vero

Weekends is a pilot to a proposed web series based on action sports and adventure. Ed and Michael from No Anchor took a trip to Vero Beach, FL to meet up with pro surfer friends Logan and Cole. After a fun filled weekend we decided to make a pilot for a possible surfer web series. Make sure to spread the word if you want to see more!

Song: FMLYBND - Electricity. No Anchor supports all kind of action sports, artists and musicians. If you or anyone you know would like to be in one of our videos, hit us up at our contact form.

Tricking in the Dark - Ashes by No Anchor

For "Ashes", we took Casey Jones, a super talented 15 year-old tricker, out in a field and got crazy at 60 FPS with our Canon DSLRs. We had a blast on set and filmed for hours without getting into trouble. We were tricking all night. Its hard finding an open field to film all night at in the city. Cool film locations aren't really Orlando's specialty, but the talent here is amazing!

Snowboarding Custom Rails and Fire - Timber by No Anchor

Snowboarding with No Anchor is a winter sport of its own. In No Anchor's snowboarding videos we log, jump cars, and light everything on fire! Check out more on our website.

All snowboard stunts are filmed in beautiful North Idaho by the No Anchor crew and team rider Lane Smith. Second snowboarder is Edwin Olding (NA Co-Owner). It's amazing what kind of abuse these boards will take, but we did break two boards in the making of this film. Lane's board by Never Summer Snowboards held up the whole time!
